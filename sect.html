SECRETARIAT'S MESSAGE

It all began in 2013 when in the heart of our school campus, a dream was woven. The dream of a teacher, the dream of the students and what would go on to be the dream of hundreds of people. From being a conference in the beginning to becoming a feeling, JMUN has truly travelled a long way.
Though it will be held online this year, JMUN has prepared a lot of surprises to carry forward the legacy of the conferences. We promise to provide an unforgettable MUN experience. We promise learning, knowledge, experience, agendas and crises that will make you rack your brains. We promise communication, cooperation, understanding, encouragement to speak your mind and make memories you will never forget.
One does not need a microphone, a room full of people and a spotlight upon them to voice out opinions; no matter the platform, one can always Deliberate, Defend and Deliver.
The Secretariat wishes luck to all the delegates and welcomes them to the 8th Edition of Jaipuria Model United Nations.
Regards, The Secretariat.
