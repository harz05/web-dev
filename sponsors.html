AMRIT SAVERA

“The crisis we face about ‘truth’ and reliable facts is predicated less on thfe ability to get people to believe the wrong thing as it is on the ability to get people to doubt the right thing.”
Jaipuria Model United Nations has been working towards bringing out the truth and delivering facts. Building upon this custom, we announce with utmost pride Amrit Savera will be partnering with Jaipuria Model United Nations.
Amrit Savera is a local newspaper that truly stands on bringing out unbiased and responsive information for the public and condemns misleading fake information and advertisements carried out for personal gain. It considers the credibility of present day media a priority and only delivers verified facts for its reader.
It digitally circulates its newspaper across the cities of Lucknow and Hardoi in the central as well as remote areas and persistently tries to improve as per the feedback of the public.
Amidst this prolonged situation of unrest in our country, Amrit Savera has tried to mitigate the upheavals by disseminating widespread information on COVID-19 and creating awareness among the people by establishing facts given by eminent people in the field of health and circulating valuable information coming from the government.
Amrit Savera hopes to reinforce its aspirations by contributing to Jaipuria Model United Nations and we shall make use of this opportunity judiciously to further build upon our objective of a free and fair press and the upliftment of our fraternity as a whole.
Regards,
The Secretariat.
