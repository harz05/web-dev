Principal's Message

Mrs. PROMINI CHOPRA

Dear Aspirants,
Human beings must have an aim, a lofty aim, if the human race has to evolve. On the quality of the aim, the resolute will and perseverance to realise the aim, depends our progress and the quality of our life.
Those who conceived ‘The United Nations’ at the end of World War II had a high, wide and generous aim of raising the consciousness of the world towards Human Rights, Peace and Security. They could perceive the consequential progress and development of nations. Despite all the threats at the boundaries of nations, a realisation to refrain from war rules the globe in the present time. Thanks to the United Nations!
JMUN and The United Nations are cognate in the loftiness of their aim. Whatever be our ideal, it cannot be realised unless you and us imagine, organise, internalise, create and collaborate to arrive at perfect solutions in our committees. Your excellence and accomplishments will be our honour for they are essential for us to reach our aim. Together we grow! Replete with opportunities to take initiatives and think critically, deliberate over global problems to arrive at inclusive collaborative solutions, JMUN invites insightful thinking minds and impactful speakers who can accept and abnegate assumptions with equanimity.
We invite you to find your sweet spot as you play your role in the joyfully educative, stimulating, diplomatic simulation.
The JMUN experience awaits you.
Best wishes.
Promini Chopra
Principal.
